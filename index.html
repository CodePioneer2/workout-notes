Bodybuilder Diet Plan for Muscle Growth

Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (7:00 AM)

  • 6 egg whites + 2 whole eggs (240g egg whites, 100g whole eggs)
  • 1 cup of oatmeal with berries (80g oatmeal, 100g berries)
  • 1 banana (120g)
  • 1 cup of low-fat milk (240ml)

Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM)

  • Greek yogurt with honey and a handful of mixed nuts (200g yogurt, 20g honey, 30g nuts)
  • 1 apple (150g)

Lunch (1:00 PM)

  • Grilled chicken breast (225g)
  • Quinoa (185g cooked)
  • Steamed broccoli and carrots (150g broccoli, 100g carrots)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (14g for cooking)

Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM)

  • Protein shake (1 scoop whey protein, 30g)
  • Whole grain sandwich with lean turkey breast and avocado (100g turkey, 50g avocado)

Pre-Workout Meal (5:30 PM)

  • Brown rice (200g cooked)
  • Lean beef or chicken (170g)
  • Steamed vegetables (spinach, kale, 150g total)

Post-Workout Meal (8:00 PM)

  • Protein shake (2 scoops whey protein, 60g)
  • 1 banana (120g)

Dinner (9:00 PM)

  • Baked salmon (225g)
  • Sweet potato (200g)
  • Mixed green salad with olive oil dressing (100g salad, 14g olive oil)

Evening Snack (11:00 PM)

  • Cottage cheese (1 cup, 200g)
  • Handful of almonds (30g)

Supplements

  • Multivitamin: Once daily with breakfast
  • Creatine: 5 grams post-workout
  • Branched-Chain Amino Acids (BCAAs): During workout
  • Fish Oil: 1-2 grams daily with meals

Training Plan for Muscle Growth

Weekly Split (6 Days Training, 1 Day Rest)

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Chest Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extension: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 2: Back and Biceps

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Bent Over Rows: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Barbell Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 3: Legs

  • Squats: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12-15 reps per leg
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps

Day 4: Shoulders

  • Military Press: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Front Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Rear Delt Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Shrugs: 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 5: Arms

  • Barbell Curls: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Skull Crushers: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Cable Curls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 6: Full Body/Conditioning

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Clean and Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1-minute hold

Day 7: Rest

  • Focus on stretching, mobility work, and active recovery.

Notes

  • Warm-Up: Always start with a 10-15 minute warm-up to prepare your muscles and joints for the workout.
  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase the weights you lift to ensure continuous muscle growth.
  • Rest: Ensure adequate rest between sets (1-2 minutes for hypertrophy training).
  • Hydration: Stay well-hydrated throughout the day and especially during workouts.
